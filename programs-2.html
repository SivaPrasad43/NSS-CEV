Chuvadu

Date : 21/12/2018 - 27/12/2018

A 7 day special camp was conducted from Paloor L.P School, Vilangad. The main aim was to build bunds across two water bodies which is helpful for storing water during summer. We did it successfully. We had painted the dangerous dips on the road, done electrical works of many houses over there, filled grow bags for their cultivation, etc. Finally we had a forest study with the villagers.

Flood Relief Survey

Date : 15/9/2018 - 16/9/2018

As a part of flood relief activity, an online survey was conducted at Thurayoor Panchayath (Kozhikode District) on 15th and 16th September as per the request from the Collectorate, using the application “Rebuild Kerala”.9 volunteers from our college participated for the survey. Panchayath President (Thurayoor Panchayath) co-ordinated the survey, and each ward members helped us a lot for doing the survey.
