Dheekshana

Date : 4/1/2020 - 10/1/2020

The sumptuous journey of NSS volunteers of 2019-21 is a forefront delight to ourselves. For the first time in history this year NSS camp DHEEKSHANA of College of engineering vadakara was held online due to the unexpected epidemic and it was a spectacular experience for all our active volunteers. The Motto of NSS "Not Me But You"is a terse expression.on face it appears to be very simple and short.but behind it lies a lot of meaning. It is very deep and suggestive and is keeping with the spirit of service for others. Engineering Vadakara(Unit No:677),aims to always include this Motto in the day- to-day program.The seven day camp forms an integral part of NSS. It aim to help young people to reach their Full social, economic and human potential,while instilling a strong sense of civic engagement facilitating greater youth participation in achieving sustainable development goals. This year the Camp was conducted from 04-01-2021 to 10-01-2021 through online with some offline activities like cleaning, kitchen renovation, campus beautification and documentation work nearer to the area of school and panchayat.

Chuvadu

Date : 21/12/2018 - 27/12/2018

A 7 day special camp was conducted from Paloor L.P School, Vilangad. The main aim was to build bunds across two water bodies which is helpful for storing water during summer. We did it successfully. We had painted the dangerous dips on the road, done electrical works of many houses over there, filled grow bags for their cultivation, etc. Finally we had a forest study with the villagers.

Punarjjani

Date : 5/9/2017 - 11/9/2017

“Punarjjani” is one of the dream project of NSS Technical Cell. Most of the furniture and bio medical equipment in government hospitals are damaged and kept aside due to lack of timely repairing or caring. The NSS units of the college selected “Punarjjani” as the project of this year’s annual seven day camp. The hospital selected for activity was District Govt.Hospital Vadakara. The scheme that aim at improving the basic infrastructure of District Govt.hospital Vadakara save up to RS=1000000 by repairing the existing infrastructure. The student started their Mission from August 5 and successfully completed on August 11 2017. Project like this are needed which while benefiting the state enhance the skill capabilities of young engineers.
