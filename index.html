About NSS CEV

National Service Scheme of College of Engineering Vadakara was initiated on the year of 2017. During the year of 2018-19 CEV NSS Unit was under the Technical cell. After that it came under APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University. National Service Scheme aims to instilling the idea of social welfare in students, and to provide service to society without bias. By involving many social activities the volunteers are building their personality. Arjun Nambiar and Aiswarya, Midhun Radh V T and Arsha K, Adil and Arunima Satheesh M S, Muhammad Javad V V and Neeshma A P, Karthik C R and Hridhya Jayaraj are the volunteers Secretaries of the year 2017-18, 2018-19, 2019-20, 2020-2021, 2021-21 respectively. CEV NSS Unit is developing in many ways by conducting different varieties of programs and events either in online or offline mood.
